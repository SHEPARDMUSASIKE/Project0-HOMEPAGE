THE POPULATION OF ZIMBABWE

juncle life, Dec 7, 2017

The current population of Zimbabwe is 14,699,286 as of Monday, September 30, 2019, based on Worldometers elaboration of the latest United Nations data. Zimbabwe 2019 population is estimated at 14,645,468 people at mid year according to UN data. Zimbabwe population is equivalent to 0.19% of the total world population.
